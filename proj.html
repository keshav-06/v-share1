V-Share: Together We Share

We are V-Share we provide following services:
1. Sharing of materials among students.
2. Cab sharing and car pooling.
3. Easy and fast delivery.

1. Sharing With Seniors

We provide a interface for the freshers and other juniors who can use their senior's materials at affordable prices without buying new things.
This helps both seniors and juniors to share their materials.

2. Cab Share/Car pooling

We are providing a place where different people who wants to go to same place at same time can come and meet and share their Taxi/Cab
This makes taxi/cab affordable for all and also a step towards saving the environment.

3. Delivery Service

We provide easy and fast delivery service of everything you arder at a very affordable price. We deliver whatever you order, wherever you order, whenever you order in your campus
